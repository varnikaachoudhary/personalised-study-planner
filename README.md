Personalized Study Planner is an application designed to help students effectively organize and manage their study schedules based on individual goals, priorities, and deadlines. It allows users to plan daily and weekly tasks, set reminders, and track their progress over time, ensuring better time management and productivity. The system helps reduce procrastination by providing a structured approach to studying and balancing multiple subjects or activities. It focuses on improving consistency and efficiency by enabling users to customize their schedules according to their learning pace and preferences. Overall, the project demonstrates practical problem-solving skills and highlights the importance of structured planning in achieving academic success.
